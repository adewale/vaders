## Sound Effects

All sound effects (*.wav) were procedurally generated using ffmpeg for this project.
They are original works and released into the public domain (CC0).

- `shoot.wav` - Laser blip (880Hz sine, 50ms)
- `alien_killed.wav` - Descending tone with vibrato (440Hz, 100ms)
- `player_died.wav` - Low pink noise explosion (300ms)
- `wave_complete.wav` - Ascending C-E-G-C arpeggio (500ms)
- `game_over.wav` - Descending G-E-C sad tones (800ms)
- `menu_select.wav` - High click (1200Hz, 30ms)
- `menu_navigate.wav` - Soft tick (800Hz, 20ms)
- `countdown_tick.wav` - Beep (660Hz, 100ms)
- `game_start.wav` - C-E-G-C fanfare (600ms)
- `ufo.wav` - Warbling tone with vibrato/tremolo (500ms)
